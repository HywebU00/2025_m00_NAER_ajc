Journal of Textbook Research

Journal of Textbook Research, founded in 2008, is a leading scholarly journal in the Chinese-speaking world dedicated to the study of textbooks. It is published tri-annually in mid-April, August, and December, featuring articles on the development of textbooks and teaching materials. Submissions are welcome and are reviewed on a rolling basis.

  • Frequency: Tri-annually (three issues per year)
  • Indexed in: TSSCI
  • ISSN: 1999-8856 (Print)/ 1999-8864 (Online)

一、發刊宗旨
《教科書研究》（以下稱本刊）為國家教育研究院定期發行之專業期刊，登載與教科書及教材相關之議題、政策及實務興革等研究成果。希冀透過本刊之發行，建立對話平臺，促進教科書研究者與實務工作者之互動交流，發展教科書制度與實務研究，作為本院推動教科書相關政策之學術研究基礎，以提昇教科書及教材之品質。
二、出刊方式及頻率
