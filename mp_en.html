Journal of Textbook Research

《Journal of Textbook Research》founded in 2008, is a leading scholarly journal in the Chinese-speaking world dedicated to the study of textbooks. It is published tri-annually in mid-April, August, and December, featuring articles on the development of textbooks and teaching materials.

Education Trends E-Journal Introduction

The Education Trends E-Journal is a practice-oriented electronic publication with a multi-faceted mission. It integrates and analyzes educational data, issues, and strategies; disseminates research findings; and serves to communicate educational information while highlighting the unique characteristics of various schools.
